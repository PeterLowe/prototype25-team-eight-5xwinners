# The Ghastly Grab

The Ghastly Grab is a point-and-click horror game where your objective is to loot a creepy and abandoned house by clicking on precious items. But be careful! Clicking
the wrong items or clicking randomly can cause noise disturbances that can awaken a ghost. You can make use of your bag to figure out what you need and how you might access them.


Will you be able to find all the valuables without making too much noise or will you fall victim to the slumbering ghost?



All SFX are Foley or nature sounds. All art assets were also made by hand. The menu music is [The Piano Before Cynthia (Cynthia's Theme)](https://youtu.be/uZFROsvADpo).
